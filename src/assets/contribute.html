Design

  • Overhauling our Website

    Our project's website as it stands today is a Wordpress template with many restrictions. There are a lot of things to communicate, that have no proper space.
    For example a better and more visual explanation of our vision(s), the team etc. Every person with a design background would immediately spot all the tiny mismatches in the design.

    Help us having a professional looking website for our project.
    If that sounds like something for you, write us an email at info@worldbrain.io
