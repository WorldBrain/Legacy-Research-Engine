How to use this extension
  • Follow our progress on Patreon

Searching for keywords

Regular search:

  1. Type in w + space/tab + your search terms into the address bar.
  2. The result are marked in the drop-down with the icon in the beginning.

Exclude words:

-"funny meme" related "to cats"

Searches for websites you visited containing the keywords “related” and “to cats”, and without the phrase “funny meme”.

Filtering by time(span)

Build your queries like that to filter by time:

before: "yesterday at 5pm" after: "three weeks ago" misinformation blog "critical thinking"

Searches for websites that you visited between yesterday at 5pm and 3 weeks ago containing the keywords “misinformation” and “blog” and “critical thinking”

critical thinking after: "2 weeks ago"

Searches for websites you visited in the last 2 weeks containing the keywords “critical” and “thinking”

climate change after:11/29/2015 before:3/26/2016

Searches for websites you visited between 11/29/2015 and 3/26/2016 containing the keywords “climate” and “change”
